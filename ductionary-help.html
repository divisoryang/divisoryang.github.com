Setting Default Search Language

There's two ways:

1. Swipe a row right in table "Search Launguage" and the row will be set as default.

2. While searching, tap "Language Button" on the right of Text Field and it will change to another language in "Search Language" group.

Deleting

You can delete histories, Search Languages by swiping the row left(that row turn red during swiping).
